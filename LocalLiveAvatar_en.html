Accessibility Technology

The product was developed as part of my project aimed at assisting people with disabilities. The goal was to create a digital twin for individuals who have lost their voice and/or suffer from visual impairments resulting from trauma.

For such individuals, I personally create a full digital clone‚Äîreplicating their voice and a personalized avatar. Afterward, using a Telegram bot, these individuals can communicate and vocalize their thoughts. For many people and their families, this holds significant psychological importance.
