AbleMouse

The Affordable & DIY Alternative to High-Tech Assistive Devices

AbleMouse is an open-source project that breaks down the barriers between a person and their computer.
Control your mouse with just tongue movements or simple touches on fully customizable pedals with any available body part.

Our motto: Affordable technology should be accessible to everyone.

Why is this a revolution?

The Democratizing Alternative

Virtually Free

Complete Independence

Works on Windows, macOS, and Android. Full cross-platform compatibility.

Open-Source & Hackable

Assemble and customize it yourself in a garage, workshop, or makerspace. All instructions and code are openly available. It's built by the community, for the community.

For Everyone

Helps both completely immobile users and those with even minimal motor skills.

Who is it for?

People with specific conditions

For people who, for various reasons, are unable to operate a regular mouse effectively (ALS (Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis), spinal cord injuries, muscular dystrophy...)

Rehabilitation patients

For those undergoing rehabilitation after a stroke.

Caregivers & advocates

For parents, teachers, and activists who want to provide immediate, affordable help to their loved ones and the community.
